SHAPE FORMULAS

    Right Triangle
  • Pythagoras Theorem: a2 + b2 = c2 Area = ½ ab Perimeter = a + b + √(a2 + b2) Where, c = hypotenuse of a triangle a = altitude of a triangle b = base of a triangle
    Triangle
  • Perimeter, P = a + b + c Area, A = ½ bh Height, h = 2(A/b) a,b,c are the sides of a triangle.
    Rectangle
  • Perimeter = 2(l + w) Area = lw Diagonal, d = √(l2 + w2) l = length of a rectangle w = width of a rectangle
    Parallelogram
  • Perimeter, P = 2(a + b) Area, A = bh Height, h = A/b Base, b = A/h a and b are the sides of a parallelogram h = height of a parallelogram
    Circle
  • Circumference = 2πr Area = πr2 Diameter = 2r r = radius of a circle
    Square
  • Perimeter, P = 4a Area, A = a2 Diagonal, d = a√2 Side, a = √A = d/2√2 a = side of a square
    Cube
  • Area, A = 6a2 Volume, V = a3 Edge, a = V⅓ Space diagonal = a√3 a = side of a cube
    Cuboid
  • Surface Area, A = 2(lb + bh + hl) Volume, V = lbh Space diagonal, d = √( l2 + b2 +h2) l= length b= breath h= height
    Cylinder
  • Total Surface Area, A = 2πrh + 2πr2 Curved Surface Area, Ac = 2πrh Volume, V = πr2h Base Area, Ab = πr2 Radius, r = √(V/πh) r= radius of a cylinder h= height of a cylinder
    Cone
  • Total Surface Area, A = πr(r+l) = πr[r+√(h2+r2)] Curved Surface Area, Ac = πrl Volume, V = ⅓πr2h Slant Height, l = √(h2+r2) Base Area, Ab = πr2 r= radius of a cone h= height of a cone l = slant height
    Sphere
  • Surface Area, A = 4πr2 Volume, V = ⁴⁄₃πr3 Diameter = 2r Where, r= radius of a sphere
